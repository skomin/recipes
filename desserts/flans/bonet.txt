Difficulty: 7/10
Time:
  preparation: 40 minutes
  baking: 30 minutes

------------------------
Ingredients (4 people):
------------------------
400g whole milk
55g amaretti
2 spoons chocholate
4   eggs
4 + 6 spoons of sugar

----------
Procedure:
----------
4 spoons of sugar in the flan, 6 to make the caramel.
Make the caramel.
Make the flan.
